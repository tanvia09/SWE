The Valkyries

The Mount Hebron chapter of the Society of Women Engineers (SWE), the Valkyries, aims to support women in engineering and technology. Our community offers a space where women and other underrepresented genders can connect, share experiences, and empower each other.

Upcoming Events

Candy City
A space for all students interested in engineering, hosted by Hebron's SWE chapter. Celebrate spring and learn engineering skills by designing and building structures made entirely out of candy. Open to all skill levels — no experience needed!
